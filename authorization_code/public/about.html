About Us

We are a group of guys, from Texas and Georgia, that like to listen to music while we code. Our first project idea was using an api to help with the paradox of choice we all find ourselves in. That project sadly came to a screaching halt when we we "suspended" from using the API. So we came together in the litle time we had left to complete it and decided we would just restart, and go for the notoriously easy path of using Spotify Api.

About Tucker:

I currently live in Rocky Face, Georgia. I am a Staff Sergeant in the Georgia National Guard, where I am a Senior OPS Advisor at B Co 1/54th SFAB out of Fort Benning, Ga. For most of 2019 I was deployed to Nangarhar, Afghanistan, where I worked and ran missions along side a team from 10th Special Forces Group. There I served as a machine gun team leader, until I got promoted and was given the responsibility of an 11 man squad. Shortly after that deployment I went to Texas to work along side the Border Patrol. After that I went to Elite Lineman Training Institute to become a lineman, where I got second in our class rodeo, and won honor graduate. While working as an underground apprentice my passion for progrmaming had been rekindled due to going back through a Python 3 project book. Shortly after finishing the book I started back to school at Digital Crafts.
